Cookies Policy

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. Please review our cookies policy below:

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They allow websites to recognize your device, store certain information about your preferences, and improve your browsing experience. Cookies are used across the web to provide tailored experiences, remember login details, and analyze visitor behavior.

Each cookie is unique to your browser and may contain anonymous information such as a unique identifier, website name, and some digits and numbers. Cookies cannot access or retrieve data from your device’s files or install malware.

2. How We Use Cookies

At E Travel City, we use cookies to make our website functional and user-friendly. Our primary reasons for using cookies include:

  • Analyzing Website Traffic: Cookies help us track how visitors navigate through our website, which pages are popular, and where improvements can be made. This data helps us optimize user experience and enhance the website’s functionality.
  • Personalizing Content: By remembering your preferences and interests, cookies enable us to provide content that is relevant to you. This includes personalized travel recommendations, based on past site visits or saved preferences.
  • Targeted Advertising: Cookies allow us to display ads relevant to your interests, including offers and promotions based on browsing behavior. Third-party ad providers may also use cookies to deliver targeted ads.
  • Understanding User Interaction: Cookies help us see how users interact with different sections of our website, allowing us to improve accessibility, functionality, and layout based on user feedback and behavior patterns.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Our website uses different types of cookies for various purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the core functions of our website. They enable basic features, such as secure logins, and ensure that pages load correctly. Without these cookies, parts of the website may not function properly.
  • Analytical Cookies: These cookies gather information about visitor interactions with our site. By analyzing data on page visits, duration, and other patterns, we can improve our website’s usability and functionality.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies remember your choices, such as language preferences or login details, to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies collect data about your browsing habits to deliver ads relevant to your interests. We may work with third-party providers who set their own cookies to display ads aligned with your preferences.
  • Session Cookies: These are temporary cookies that expire once you close your browser. They are mainly used for the duration of your visit to ensure smooth navigation across our website.
  • Persistent Cookies: Persistent cookies remain on your device until they expire or are manually deleted. They help us remember your preferences and settings for future visits.

4. Managing Your Cookies

You have control over the cookies stored on your device and can manage or delete them through your browser settings. Here’s how you can control cookies:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to view, delete, or block cookies from specific or all websites. Please refer to your browser’s help section to learn how to adjust these settings.
  • Opting Out of Advertising Cookies: You can opt out of targeted advertising cookies by visiting the opt-out pages of third-party ad networks or by adjusting your ad preferences directly with providers.
  • Cookie Preferences on Our Website: We provide options for users to manage their cookie preferences directly on our website, where applicable. You may accept or reject non-essential cookies based on your preferences.

Disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on our site, and some features may not function as intended. Essential cookies, which are necessary for website performance, cannot be disabled.

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us.
